# OTSL HR & Administration Quick Reference

## Timesheets
- Hourly employees and interns submit timesheets in the HR portal by Friday 17:00 CT each week.
- Time is recorded in 15-minute increments. Overtime requires prior supervisor approval.
- Late timesheets delay payment to the following pay cycle.

## Time Off (PTO)
- Full-time employees accrue 10 days of PTO in year one, 15 days after year three.
- PTO requests are submitted in the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance for planned leave.
- Interns are unpaid for days not worked but must still notify their supervisor 48 hours ahead for planned absences.

## New Hire & Intern Onboarding
Day 1 checklist:
1. Badge photo and building access at the front desk (1133 South Madison Ave).
2. IT setup: laptop, email, TMS account, and Slack access (submit ticket to it@otsl.com if anything is missing).
3. Safety orientation — required before entering any warehouse floor.
4. Review the SOP handbook and sign the acknowledgment form in the HR portal within the first week.

## Warehouse Safety Basics
- High-visibility vests are required on all warehouse floors at all times.
- Pedestrians yield to forklifts; use marked walkways only.
- Report any incident or near-miss to your supervisor the same day — no exceptions.

## Expense Reimbursement
- Submit expenses with receipts in the HR portal within 30 days of purchase.
- Mileage for approved business travel is reimbursed at the current IRS rate.
- Purchases over $200 require manager pre-approval.
